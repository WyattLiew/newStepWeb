Facial recognition kiosk with temperature measurements

Features

  • 8inch, 1280 x 800, non-touch
  • Android system, 2G RAM, 8G ROM
  • Facial recognition
  • Temperature Measurement accuracy 99.8%
  • Support 16 languages
  • Free temperature measurement software
  • Provide API/SDK development

Product Specification

  • It can measure body temperature while identifying the person’s name, and the system would process the result, say if it detects a temperature over 37.3 degrees
  • It can identify everyone in a crowd of up to 30 people “within a second”. Support 30,000 ID database, the success rate for people without a mask is about 99.7%. Even when wearing a mask, the recognition rate can reach about 95%, which can ensure that most people can be identified
  • 8inch IPS Panel, industrial grade design, ip65
  • Using an industrial binocular wide dynamic camera, night infrared and LED dual fill light
  • ANDROID system, RK3288, quad-core, 2G RAM, 8G ROM
  • SUPPORT ID card reader, fingerprinter, IC card reader, QR code scanner, etc. Provide API/SDK for software development
